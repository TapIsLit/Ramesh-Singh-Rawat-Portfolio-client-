About Our Organization

Devbhumi Mind Care Welfare Society

We are dedicated to promoting mental health awareness and providing professional psychological counseling and rehabilitation services across Uttarakhand and beyond.

Our mission is to create mentally healthy, emotionally resilient, and career-oriented individuals through evidence-based psychological services, counseling, and compassionate care.

Our Services

Mental Health Counseling

Professional counseling for stress management, anxiety disorders, depression, emotional difficulties, and behavioral challenges.

Psychotherapy Services

Individual psychotherapy, cognitive behavioral interventions, emotional regulation techniques, and supportive psychotherapy.

Career Counseling

Career assessment, subject selection guidance, competitive examination guidance, and professional development coaching.

Rehabilitation Psychology

Disability counseling, rehabilitation planning, family counseling, and community rehabilitation programs.

De-Addiction Services

Tobacco, nicotine, alcohol and drug addiction counseling with relapse prevention and family support programs.

School Mental Health Programs

Mental health awareness, life skills education, examination stress management, and bullying prevention programs.
